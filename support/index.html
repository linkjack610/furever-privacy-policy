Data Deletion Requests

Last updated: 26 June 2025

We respect your privacy and provide a way for users to request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

  1. Email allergenhubstore@gmail.com from the address linked to your account.
  2. Include a clear request to delete your Allergen Hub account and related data.
  3. We may ask for identity verification before completing the request.

Once verified, we will process deletion within 30 days, subject to legal retention obligations.

Scope of Assistance

Allergen Hub is designed to assist your internal review workflow. It is not a substitute for your regulatory compliance process, legal advice, or formal allergen declarations.
