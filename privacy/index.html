Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2025

Allergen Hub (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Allergen Hub mobile app (“the App”).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and profile details you provide through account features.
  • User Content: Product records, ingredient notes, and related content entered in the App.
  • Usage Data: Device type, operating system, app usage events, and crash diagnostics.
  • Notification Data: Notification tokens used to deliver reminder functionality where enabled.
  • Analytics Data: Product and performance analytics used to improve app quality and reliability.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App and its features.
  • To support account access and secure product record handling.
  • To improve reliability, usability, and performance.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To process subscriptions and purchases through Google Play (we do not receive your full card details).

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party providers for app infrastructure, analytics, messaging, and payment processing. These providers may process data under their own privacy policies and may process data outside your country of residence.

4. Data Sharing & Transfers

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with service providers where required to operate the App, to comply with legal obligations, or in connection with a business transfer such as a merger or acquisition.

5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the App and comply with legal obligations.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. No method of storage or transmission is fully secure, but we take appropriate steps to reduce risk.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data, and to withdraw consent where applicable.

To exercise your rights, contact us at allergenhubstore@gmail.com.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. If we learn that personal information from a child under 13 has been collected, we will take steps to delete it.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email allergenhubstore@gmail.com.
